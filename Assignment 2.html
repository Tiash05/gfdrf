HISTORY

Methodist Girls’ High School Mamfe has a rich legacy of academic excellence, discipline, and spiritual growth.

The school was founded in 1984 and is located in Mamfe in the Eastern Region of Ghana.

Methodist Girls’ High School offers secondary education for girls and is affiliated with the Methodist Church.

Also known as MEGHIS, the school’s motto, “Honesty, Integrity, Industry,” reflects its commitment to building well-rounded individuals grounded in strong moral values.

ABOUT

Methodist Girls’ High School (MEGHIS) in Mamfe is known for providing a conducive environment for both academic and extracurricular activities. Below is a general description of some key facilities in the school:

Classrooms: Spacious and well-ventilated, designed to provide a comfortable learning atmosphere. They are equipped with desks, chairs, and blackboards or whiteboards for effective teaching.

Library: Stocked with a variety of books, from academic textbooks to novels and reference materials. It provides a quiet space for students to read and conduct research.

Dining Hall: A large hall where students gather for their meals. It provides a space for students to have breakfast, lunch, and dinner.

Sports Facilities: The school offers a variety of sports facilities, including a football field, volleyball court, and a basketball court, encouraging physical activity and teamwork.

Assembly Hall: A large hall used for gatherings, events, and school functions such as assemblies, debates, and cultural activities.

Dormitories: The boarding facilities are clean and well-maintained, offering comfortable accommodation for students. They are supervised to ensure discipline and order.

Milestones and Growth:

In its early years, MEGHIS was known for its rigorous academic curriculum, which attracted bright young girls from all over Ghana. Over time, the school has consistently ranked among the top-performing schools in the country in the West African Senior School Certificate Examination (WASSCE).

The school has continually expanded its facilities to cater to its growing student population. From modern science laboratories and computer labs to spacious dormitories and a well-stocked library, MEGHIS has evolved into a well-rounded educational institution.

The introduction of various clubs, societies, and extracurricular activities has enhanced the holistic development of students.
